FAQs

The GIIS Creator’s Summit is a platform for GIIS students to showcase their talents and ideas in the form of making a product to improve their surroundings or to resolve some problem that exists in the world.
Participants will innovate, design, engineer and make a product which will solve some issue that plagues the world today
The event will provide an amazing opportunity to learn about various new ideas and relating techniques to develop their ideas into finished products, along with valuable guidance from the GIIS Creators Club members as well as the other Clubs that participate in the Summit! No prior experience is required!
The option themes include Security, Toward a Greener Tomorrow and Space exploration, extra reference material will be provided for each of these topics to help participants better understand these topics!
The prizes will be announced soon!
To participate, please fill out the google form and accept the E-Consent on mygiis
This event will be held physically at the GIIS PG SMART Campus!
