Who We Are

The Creators’ Club! Bringing the world’s finest and latest in groundbreaking technology into our lives. Here at the Creators’ club we use design, additive manufacturing, mechanics and engineering to make a change in our community. There are 3 major parts of the club—Design, 3-D Printing and Robotics—which work together in order to create awe-inspiring projects and STEM based activities, to garner interest and attention in the area of CAD and Robotics.

"Our mission here at GIIS Creators Club is to incite innovation"

Executive Board

Goals of the club

The goal of the club is to introduce and educate our peers about future technology, by allowing them to practically use and learn this technology, including the actual hardware and software behind it .

The Creators’ Club will support various projects/teams(for competitions), as well as conduct events propagating our core values including workshops, courses, and competitions that will attract students to the Makers’ Movement.

The Creators’ Club aims to not only create but also educate, and inspire the inner makers in us.

Our Plans

Not only members but ALL participants shall be learning their fair share of application of technical skills. It will be a fun experience for especially tech savvy/inventive students who love tinkering with machines and enjoy designing and coding. We support the Makers’ Movement and wish to educate others of the same. Some of the activities we plan to pursue are as follows:

A lot of friendly competitions, particularly in the fields of Innovation and Robotics

Events that would bring people together to brainstorm ideas and solutions to real-world problems faced on the daily

Extensive training and assistance for competitions(only for students who decide to join the club)

Hands-on guidance in anything related to CAD/CAM/Robotics
